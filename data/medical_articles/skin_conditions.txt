A rash is a noticeable change in the texture or color of your skin. Itchy red rashes on the arm are often caused by contact dermatitis, which occurs when the skin comes into direct contact with an allergen or irritant. Common irritants include certain soaps, cosmetics, fragrances, jewelry, or plants like poison ivy. Treatment typically involves identifying and avoiding the trigger, and applying topical corticosteroids to reduce inflammation and itching. If the rash is accompanied by a severe fever or spreads rapidly, medical attention is required.
